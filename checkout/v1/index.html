Most Boards are Damaging Your Health & Knives

A cutting board is the unsung hero of your kitchen. Yet, the wrong material creates a silent health risk and dulls your knives daily.
The rule is simple: the more you use a tool, the higher its quality should be.

carpenter Wood/Bamboo

Even invisible cuts in natural boards become a breeding ground for mold and bacteria, silently contaminating every meal you cook. They also soak up juices, leading to foul odors and unsightly stains.

recycling Plastic/Epicurean

Many plastic boards contain BPAs, phthalates, or other toxic chemicals that can leech into food as you cut. Plus, plastic scratches easily, creating tiny crevices where bacteria can accumulate and thrive.

grid_view Glass/Stone/Steel

While these materials may seem hygienic, their extreme hardness wrecks your knives. Every cut rapidly dulls your blades, forcing you to sharpen or replace them far more often wasting time, effort, and money.
